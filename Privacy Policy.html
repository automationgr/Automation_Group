Privacy Policy

Your data protection and privacy are fundamental to how we operate.

1. Introduction

Automation Group Ltd (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.automationgroup.rw, engage our engineering and automation services, or communicate with us. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the Rwanda Data Protection Act and, where relevant, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Data: Name, job title, organization, professional credentials.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, postal address, country.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies, and usage analytics when you interact with our website.
  • Project & Enquiry Data: Information you provide in contact forms, consultation requests, or project briefs, including technical data related to your facility or project.
  • Marketing Data: Your preferences in receiving communications from us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • To respond to your enquiries, proposals, and consultation requests.
  • To deliver metering, tank gauging, fire & gas safety, energy, building management, SCADA, service & repair, and power systems solutions.
  • To manage our contractual obligations with industrial clients, manufacturers, and partners.
  • To improve our website, services, and client experience through analytics.
  • To send you newsletters, technical resources, and event invitations (only where you have opted in or as permitted by law).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations in Rwanda and other jurisdictions where we operate.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To perform a contract with you or your organization.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our engineering services, secure our systems, and market our expertise responsibly.
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly agreed to receive marketing communications.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent or at your direction.
  • With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, email platforms) who assist us in operating our website and delivering services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Where required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets — we will notify you of any change in control.

Any technical or project data you share for engineering purposes remains strictly confidential and is used solely for the agreed scope of work.

6. International Data Transfers

As a firm serving clients across East Africa, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your home jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in line with this Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law (e.g., for tax, legal, or project audit purposes). Typically, project-related data is retained for 7 years following project completion. Enquiry data is kept for up to 24 months unless it leads to a client relationship.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. These include encryption, secure servers, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure — you share data at your own risk.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to direct marketing or processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info.automationgroup@gmail.com or using the details in Section 13. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for site functionality.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to professional organizations and adult representatives. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., manufacturer partners, client websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.
