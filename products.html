Product 02

Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) With the Highest Availability

A range comprised of modules with power of 10, 20, 25, 50 and 60 kW, which can be accommodated in single racks with total power ranging from 20 to 600 kW. The power racks can be extended to 3.6 MW by horizontally paralleling up to 60 modules.

By eliminating every single point of failure, adding active-redundant technology, preventing human error, and reducing the time to maintain and repair, our UPS delivers an industry-leading availability of 9 nines to fulfil the needs of the most critical power applications.

  • Triple-mode parallel bus — three independent communication lines and electronic circuits eliminate single points of failure.
  • Full Bypass redundancy — no centralized critical components like a Single Bypass Architecture.
  • Minimized MTTR — in-frame per-module bypass fuses re-establish the system fast after an output short-circuit.
  • Safe Smart Battery Test — battery monitoring, tests, and health diagnosis without mains disconnection.
  • Hot-Swappable — modules can be swapped without switching the load to bypass, minimizing human error.

Product 03

Power and Control Systems Cabinet Fabrication

We design, assemble, and power up electrical power and control cabinets — producing and implementing electrical design (drawings): cabinet GA, power single line diagram, internal wiring diagram, instrument cable block diagram, and loop diagram.

We also generate the engineering documents needed for a successful handover: instruments data sheet, FAT/SAT procedures, control philosophy, and assessment reports — performing project setups using established engineering and design standards.
