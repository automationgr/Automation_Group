About Apex R&M Group

A premier spatial consulting firm founded on the conviction that spatial intelligence is the backbone of sustainable development across Africa and the world.

Our Story

Born from a Vision to Transform Africa Through Spatial Intelligence

Apex R&M Group was founded in 2025 in Kigali, Rwanda at the heart of one of Africa's most ambitious development stories. Our founders recognised a critical gap: Africa's growing infrastructure, land, and environmental challenges demanded a truly African spatial consulting firm one that combined global technical excellence with deep local knowledge.

Unlike traditional consulting firms that parachute expertise into African contexts, we are embedded in the continent's realities. We understand the governance structures, the land tenure systems, the regulatory frameworks, and most importantly the communities whose lives our work directly shapes.

Today, Apex R&M Group brings together multidisciplinary expertise in GIS, remote sensing, land management, environmental consultancy, and infrastructure planning delivering solutions that are technically sound, contextually relevant, and measurably impactful.

Our Mission

Mission Statement

"To empower governments, organisations, and communities with cutting-edge spatial intelligence, resource management expertise, and strategic advisory services driving sustainable planning and development, evidence-based decision-making, and measurable impact across Africa and the world."
