Our Story

Automate…Renovate

Automation Group Ltd is a professional systems integrator based in Kigali, Rwanda — East Africa, offering services of engineering, supply, installation, commissioning, and repair for process and industrial automation.

We get full range of premium industrial services for your business: design and development, procurement, installation and commissioning of Control systems, Flow metering systems, Tank Gauging Systems, Integrated Fire & Gas and Safety systems, and more.

Our mission is to increase the competitiveness of our clients through modernization of their facilities — our main concerns are Efficiency, Quality, and Plant Safety, combining the newest technologies with our knowledge and innovative approach to problem solving.

How We Work

Our Approach

From basic design to commissioning and start-up — a structured, transparent process for every project.

1

Design

Producing and implementing electrical and process design — GA drawings, single line diagrams, internal wiring, instrument cable block diagrams, and loop diagrams.

2

Procure

Sourcing electrical and mechanical spare parts, instruments, controllers, and equipment from a wide range of trusted manufacturers.

3

Install

Field installation of metering skids, tank gauging systems, fire & gas safety systems, SCADA, and power & control cabinets.

4

Commission

FAT/SAT procedures, control philosophy validation, full loop tests, and project setups using established engineering standards.

5

Support

Preventive maintenance, calibration, training, and service & repair to keep every system running at its best.
